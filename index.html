Our Epic Stops

Day 1: Parys to Harrismith

180 km

Highlights

  • Breakfast at Die Pont - R120 pp (amazing river view)
  • Sterkfontein Dam - Free entry (perfect photo spot)
  • Lunch at Royal Danish - R150 pp (best burgers!)
  • Harrismith Lodge - R800 per room (comfy beds)

Day 2: Harrismith to Howick

220 km

Highlights

  • Howick Falls - Free (breathtaking views)
  • Midmar Dam watersports - R250 pp (epic tubing!)
  • Dinner at Piggly Wiggly - R180 pp (great craft beer)
  • Howick Hotel - R950 per room (historic charm)

Day 3: Howick to Durban

220 km

Highlights

  • Ushaka Marine World - R195 pp (shark cage diving!)
  • Moses Mabhida Stadium - R150 pp (awesome views)
  • Dinner at Florida Road - R220 pp (vibey nightlife)
  • Beachfront Airbnb - R1200 per room (ocean view)
